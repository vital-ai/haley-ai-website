Haley A.I. as a Service

Haley AI-as-a-Service provides the operational infrastructure for your Haley implementation, in a pay per usage model. The usage is determined by the number and complexity of actions Haley performs for your business. This is a combination of the number of messages processed and generated, the amount of data, the number of predictive models, and frequency of data processing for training predictive models.
